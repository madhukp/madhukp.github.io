About this visualization


Objective:

The objective of this project is to provide a visual narration on "Road Accident Deaths in India" and help users find answers to questions like -
     •  What was the trend in death counts from road accidents over years?
     •  How did the numbers compare Gender wise? Did any gender dominate the death toll counts?
     •  Did any particular region or states consistently report higher cases over years?
     •  To what age groups did the victims belong to? Are numbers for any age group concering?
     •  Should traffic/licensing rules for any particular mode of transportation be relooked at?
